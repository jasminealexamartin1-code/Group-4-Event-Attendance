Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I register for an event? ▼

Browse our events page, select an event, and click the registration button. Fill out the form and you'll receive a confirmation email.

Can I cancel my registration? ▼

Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the event. Contact us with your registration details and we'll process the cancellation.

Do I need to bring anything to the event? ▼

You'll receive a confirmation email with a QR code. Simply show this at check-in. Some events may have specific requirements mentioned in the confirmation email.

Are virtual events recorded? ▼

Most virtual events are recorded and made available to registered attendees after the event. Check the event details for specific recording policies.
